## Digest

The source proposes an entity-first personal operating environment in which people, documents, conversations, places, devices, projects, and services are durable objects rather than app-owned silos. A global action layer exposes capabilities to both people and agents. History is semantic, reversible where possible, and user-owned. The current architecture retains these product goals while replacing absolute promises with provider contracts, provenance, consent, and measurable coverage.

**Adopted:** entity graph; action/provider model; global semantic history; local-first authority; composable surfaces; explicit agent access.

**Constrained:** “all data” means data owned by the platform or explicitly exported by integrations; “all actions” is measured by a conformance catalog; autonomous execution is trust-tiered.

**Deferred:** production mobile hardware parity and unrestricted third-party application control.
