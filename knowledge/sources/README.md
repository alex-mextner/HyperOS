# Source and Provenance Policy

User-provided archives and publications are retained outside the public repository until copyright, confidentiality, privacy, firmware, and redistribution status are reviewed.

The public knowledge base may contain:

- original summaries and normative specifications;
- short attributed quotations within applicable limits;
- links to lawful primary sources;
- source IDs, hashes, page references, and verification status;
- descriptions of restricted evidence without redistributing the evidence itself.

A source record must distinguish public URL, local restricted artifact, vendor documentation, research paper, standard, source code, behavioural trace, and legal advice. Restricted material must never be copied into Issues, pull requests, CI logs, or public build artifacts.
