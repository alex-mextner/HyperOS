# Licensing Status

The repository is public, but publication alone does not grant an open-source licence.

Before the first tagged community release, the project will select and add:

- a licence for original source code;
- a licence for original documentation and diagrams;
- a contributor sign-off or agreement policy;
- a third-party notices and provenance register.

Until those licences are added, contributors should not assume redistribution or modification rights beyond those provided by applicable law and GitHub's Terms of Service. Third-party source archives, PDFs, firmware, vendor materials, and extracted artifacts are not published unless redistribution rights have been verified.
