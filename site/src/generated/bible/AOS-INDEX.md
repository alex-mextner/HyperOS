<a id="status-semantics"></a>

## Status Semantics

`Research-backed normative foundation` means the document states the current implementation model and is connected to evidence and tasks, but still requires the named specialist reviews before a production or legal claim is accepted. Historical source material remains under `sources/original/` and does not silently override current normative documents.
