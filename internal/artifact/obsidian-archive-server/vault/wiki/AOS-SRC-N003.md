## Digest

The comparison identifies the strategic strengths of iOS and Android—hardware integration, ecosystem reach, application compatibility, camera quality, power management, distribution, and security investment—while arguing for a different unit of interaction: entities and intents rather than applications.

The normative disposition is not to claim immediate parity. Agent OS separates the product hypothesis from the hardware programme, publishes measurable quality gates, and maintains multiple targets. Pixel 9 is a quality ceiling and evidence target. Documented boards are the clean portability targets. A future contract device is an architectural option, not an assumed milestone.
